    • Future of farming

      The future of farming will take place in the sea, with microalgae, or on land, with the breeding of insects or the use of new techniques such as aquaponics or permaculture (which is not so new as ca because it draws on farming techniques from the past). But this is how we will feed the world while respecting our planet. It is a very inspiring subject.

    • Standardisation, Modularisation

      I imagined many systems using modules (and even in my code) but to explain this idea I will talk about plastic: today, in France, bottles are made with many different plastics, which makes it impossible to sorting and recycling: what a loss! the same for telephones. The phonebloks for example is an incredible idea: a modular smartphone where you can change the broken parts without completely changing the phone. Making things modular and standardized makes life easier.
